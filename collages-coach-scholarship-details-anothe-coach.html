Description
The world’s most popular sport is quickly becoming a favourite in the United States as well. Football, or Soccer, as it’s called in the US, is played by men and women at schools of every size. Attending a university with a soccer scholarship will give you the chance to earn your degree while playing the sport you love.
Students seeking a soccer scholarship will need to attend our 10-day Showcase (held every July in Florida). At the Showcase, you’ll play in front of college coaches from across the United States, and have the chance to discuss their programmes and the schools they represent. After the Showcase, you will receive a detailed report of scholarship offers from at least nine schools. More information on the annual Showcase can be found

Best Time to Apply
Your scholarship offers will be valid for two years after attending a Showcase. Since soccer is an autumn sport, we recommend you begin your application at age 16 and plan to attend the annual Showcase at age 17 (during year 12). This will allow you to enroll in a university at the beginning of the autumn term and compete your first year.
